UNLIM

Creative campaign for Viva-MTS

Viva-MTS has an eminent reputation and public trust in Armenia. Therefore, we were delighted to be nominated as the creative agency for this company in 2020.

Challenge

The company approached us to inform its subscribers about an upcoming exclusive tariff that allowed them to use the Internet as much as they wanted, without limits.

The task was to present this amazing opportunity in a correspondingly exceptional way.

Solution

Solution - "Our imagination has no limits": this became the core slogan of the campaign. The highlight was the promotional music video, which is spiced with contemporary music, and expresses unlimited and unbounded imagination in which even time disappears. Through dynamic dance, the performers rapidly transform into different eras, highlighting the infinite imagination and opportunities of the tariff.

We implemented this project together with Armenian celebrities. The song was created by Artem Valter and Nare Manukyan and performed by Project 12 artists.
