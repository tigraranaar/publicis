Different and Equal

Social awareness-raising campaign

Alongside humanitarian support, World Vision Armenia implements various development projects.

The "Different and equal" project was announced to combat gender-based violence and sex-selective abortion in Armenia. Our client's wish was to inform about the project in as an engaging way as possible.

Solution

Our core idea was to underline the importance of the equal rights of boys and girls in society from the very first moment of their lives and during education, sports, daily life by stopping sex-selective abortions. Thus, through a series of posters, we highlighted the fact that equality should be present in all social spheres.

For the visual implementation we picked two colors of gender representation. To avoid stereotypical blues and pinks, green and purple were selected. By combining these two colors and the Equality sign we developed the logotype of the campaign.

Solution

We applied our logotype to different items symbolizing different situations in a child's life. They have become the key visuals of our communication posters.

The campaign's culmination was dialogue videos that refute gender stereotypes in Armenian society. The dialogue between fathers and daughters shows that there should be equality between girls and boys in cases that seem simple at first glance: games, sports, choice of profession, etc.

This campaign was extremely highly rated and received the ACT Cannes tribute in Human rights.
