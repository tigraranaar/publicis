WASTE SORTING

Awareness-Raising Campaign

In 2020, sorting bins were installed in several districts of Yerevan for the first time. Yerevan Municipality announced the start of waste sorting in the city. Residents lacked knowledge on how to sort correctly.

Some people were skeptical about this issue, and there was also a great need to inform and explain the importance of waste sorting to the public. Our main challenge was to develop a waste management awareness campaign which would highlight this issue.

Solution

To communicate the problem of the waste management to the target audience we needed to develop a bold message and eye-catching visual.

"It's time to sort the waste": this was the core message we expressed through this campaign. It simultaneously signaled the beginning of waste sorting in the city and announced its importance globally.

The shape of hourglass became the key visual for the campaign. By combining the shape with transformation of recycled items we emphasized both the urgency and the need for sorting.

This campaign served as the first and largest campaign about sorting in the city. TV, radio, print and digital media channels were used reached more than 80% of the target audience.
