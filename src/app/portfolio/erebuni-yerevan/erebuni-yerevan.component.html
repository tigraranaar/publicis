EREBUNI-YEREVAN
CELEBRATION

Campaign branding and implementation

Yerevan was founded in the 8th century BC and was initially called Erebuni. The city comes together every October to celebrate its founding, with all sorts of traditional events throughout the city. Every year's celebration has its unique theme.

Challenge

In 2019 we were honored to collaborate with Yerevan Municipality and make an engaging campaign for our city's 2801st anniversary.

Our task was to find a way to engage city residents and emphasize the day's significance.

Solution

Our ideas intersected with the concept of love, something undefeatable. Our main goal was to highlight and manifest love towards our city. As result of multiple brainstorms, we came up with the simple, yet sincere, headline "My heart Yerevan".

We formed the letters of the word Yerevan into that most recognizable symbol of love - the heart. This became the logotype of the celebration. Illustrations of the city's most influential and historical sites were developed and became the central part of the bright and warm visuals that were hung all over the city.

These visuals highlighted the importance of love and care towards the city and stressed the value of preserving it.

In between the old and the new, our city faces various problems and needs protection, warmth, and love; this was accentuated through our campaign.

This campaign had its own unique soundtrack. The song was written by famous Armenian composer Tigran Mansuryan. Based on the poetry of Karen Antashyan, this song gave an entirely new breath to the city during those festive days.
