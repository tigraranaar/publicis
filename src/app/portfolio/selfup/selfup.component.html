Selfup

Crafted branding with flames

Selfup offers a wide variety of cooking classes in the Boston area. These classes are led by chefs who excel in their particular areas of expertise.

The company needed memorable branding that would emphasize its uniqueness. Our task was to make a visual identity that would convey the cooking lessons' atmosphere, taste, and smell.

Solution

The idea for this branding may seem simple at first glance, but it stands out with its authenticity. The key element in this design is fire/flame, which symbolizes cooking and makes one feel the real taste and smell of the services offered by Selfup. The main colors used are orange and black. They stress the company's specialty and convey its warm and delicious atmosphere.

The implementation of this project was an absolute pleasure for our design department. All the elements of the identity are entirely custom-crafted. Using color sprays and stencils, brand patterns and a black charcoal background were created.

The logotype was also made out of multiple layers of stencil and paints. Natural textures were used: coal, metal, wood, paper, and cloth.

As a result, we got the design and packaging that accentuated the distinctive essence of this unique company. The final notes were the special spices and sauces with which we seasoned the visuals.
